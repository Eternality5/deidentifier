Background

Privacy and confidentiality are two interrelated factors that promote trust in healthcare environment. It promotes effective communication between patients and healthcare workers. On the other hand, health research often involves gathering, linking and analyzing information about people and their health. Without availability of data, advances in health science could be severely hampered.

Goals

  • Understand the importance of privacy, data sharing and health research
  • Understand the nature of decision support system and conformance review
  • Design, build and test effectiveness of an user interface for a decision support system
